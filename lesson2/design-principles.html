Fitt's Law

Dior

The Dior Website complies with Fitt's Law, as it has an "Order" button that allows the visitor to immediately order the item of their choice. In this example we see that the larger an object is and the closer it is, the easier it is to use.

PARC: Alignment

Pandora

The Pandora website complies with the Alignment Principle, since the elements of the page are well aligned with a reasonable reference, which improves its readability and, therefore, creates a pleasant visitor experience.

PARC: Contrast

Harry Winston

The Harry Winston website complies with the contrast principle, as it has a clear difference between foreground and background colors that helps readability. You can clearly see the contrast between the white letters and the black background that makes it more distinguishable.
